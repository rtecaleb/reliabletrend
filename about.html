Our Story

Reliable Trend Enterprises LLC began with a simple idea: build products people can actually depend on, and sell them honestly, direct to the people who use them.

Today, RTE is the parent company — the umbrella — over a growing family of independent brands. Each one focuses on doing one thing exceptionally well, while sharing the same backbone: quality you can feel, support you can reach, and a name you can trust.

What We Do

We design, build, and sell products across multiple categories through our own e-commerce stores. Rather than spreading one label thin, we give each product line its own brand and identity — all backed by RTE.

ZeroStick

Precision-modified gaming controllers built on genuine hardware. For players who want every advantage their gear can give them.

BudBox

Curated lifestyle essentials, delivered in a box worth opening. Thoughtfully selected goods, shipped direct.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted name behind the products you love — proving that "trend" and "reliable" belong in the same sentence. We grow by earning trust one customer, one order, and one brand at a time.
